How It Works

Use language as currency. Alary helps students take advantage of Berkeley's diversity by matching you with a language partner.

Exchange a language. We connect you with your language partner so that you can teach and learn a new language!

Do you love boba? We highly recommend conducting lessons while drinking boba together as a way of showing your appreciation.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • thumb_upI'm matched. Now what?

    We provide the contact information for your tutor/tutee so you can contact each other and set up a mutual time to learn! If for some reasons the match does not work out (due to scheduling conflict, etc.), email us and let us know! We will rematch you as soon as possible.

  • translateI'm matched with someone who doesn't know the language I want to learn?

    That means you are matched as a tutor! It's hard to find a perfect match where the other student wants to learn the language you can teach and knows the language you want to learn. Often we have a three-way match, where you are matched as a tutor to a student and as a tutee to another student.

  • chatWhat if my tutor/tutee isn't responding?

    Email us and we will contact your tutor/tutee. We give them 3 days to respond; after that we will rematch you with another person.

  • visibility_offI haven't heard anything from you guys since I signed up. What's going on?

    We are sorry for the inconvenience! We are trying to match a vast amount of people with a variety of needs and schedules. If you are not matched yet, it means that no tutors are available who can teach the language you want to learn. If you no longer want to be matched, email us and let us know.
